Minesweeper

Welcome to the classic Minesweeper Game! :)

You will find 3 available modes, each with increased difficulty.

  • Easy: 9x9 - 10 mines.
  • Medium: 16x16 - 40 mines.
  • Hard: 30x16 - 100 mines.

To win the game, you have to sweep all fields that dont have mines.

Each field has a number, indicating how many mines are surounding it.

Stepping on a mine is bad for your health. So, dont do that...

Choose your difficulty and have fun! :)
